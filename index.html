About

Data that serves
people and planet

I am a Geospatial Data Specialist and MEL practitioner with over 3.5 years of experience across climate resilience, food systems, and carbon markets in East Africa. My work spans satellite image classification, early warning systems, impact measurement, and communicating evidence to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Currently on fellowship placement at icipe within the BioInnovate Africa Programme (Mastercard Foundation / CIFOR-ICRAF), and completing an MSc in Agricultural Information and Communication Management at the University of Nairobi.

I have roots in Baringo County, Kenya, and bring both technical rigour and community understanding to every project I work on.
