## Featured Projects

### 🗺️ Kenya Land Use Mapping & Monitoring System

**Role:** Geospatial Analyst | **Status:** Completed

Developed a comprehensive geospatial analysis system to track land use changes across 5 counties in Kenya using satellite imagery and open-source GIS tools.

**What I Did:**
- Processed and analyzed multi-temporal satellite imagery
- Created land use classification maps
- Identified environmental change patterns
- Produced visualization reports for stakeholders

**Tools & Skills:** QGIS | Google Earth Engine | Python | Remote Sensing | Spatial Analysis

**Impact:** Provided actionable geospatial intelligence for land management decisions
